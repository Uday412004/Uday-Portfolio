About Company


Reliance Industries Limited is one of India’s largest and most influential multinational conglomerates, headquartered in Mumbai. Founded in 1966 by Dhirubhai Ambani as a small textile trading firm, the company has grown into a global powerhouse with major businesses in petrochemicals, refining, oil and gas exploration, telecommunications, retail, and digital services. Today, under the leadership of chairman Mukesh Ambani, Reliance has expanded rapidly into consumer-focused sectors through ventures like Reliance Jio and Reliance Retail, transforming India’s digital connectivity and retail landscape.
 Known for innovation, scale, and strategic investments, Reliance plays a key role in India’s economic growth and global business presence.
